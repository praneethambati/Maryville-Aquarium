About Us

Well, uh, we've got a lot of fish.

Thousands of people visit the Aquarium every year in tour groups, school and camp groups, as well as other groups. We offer group ticket prices for groups of 10 or more, as wewll as discounted bulk ticket programs for corporations, hotels and non-profit organizations. We aim to create a new generation of ocean stewards, while providing regional, national and global leadership in marine conservation. So come on in and join us!
