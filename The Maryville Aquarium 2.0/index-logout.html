Shows and Tours

At the Maryville Aquarium, we offer a variety of shows and tours for our visitors.

      Dolphin Discovery

Dolphin Discovery is home of the most loved dolphins, our staff are certified to give them the best care. With more than 6 million visitors till now, we are the #1 dolphin show in the world!

      Jellies Invasion

Jellies live in every ocean, thrive in coastal and open waters, and even live in fresh water. This stunning exhibit features nine different species of these prehistoric survivors.

      Living Seashore

Get ready to explore the myriad of creatures that inhabit your shores! From sea cucumberes to urchins, we've got a variety of aquatic flora and fauna on display.

        Shark Alley

Sharks! *insert epic Jaws theme* Explore this exhibit as you get to see God's oldest hunters at their finest! From the Great White to the smaller Sand Tiger, we have them all.

      Waterfront Park

This tour caters mainly to kids, with a set of exciting water rides, as well as cute penguins to play with. Come on in!

        Blacktip Reef

Welcome to the home of the Blacktip Sharks, a unique breed of sharks that hunt and thrive among the coral reefs. They're fun creatures, when they're not trying to eat you.
